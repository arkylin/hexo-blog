LDAP
−−with-ldap=DIR         Include LDAP support
 −−with-ldap-sasl=DIR    LDAP: Include Cyrus SASL support
 Debian/Ubuntu 需安装 libldap-2.4-2, libldap2-dev 依赖包。
 Redhat/CentOS 需安装 openldap, openldap-devel 依赖包。
